## ЁЯМ╛ The Story Behind This Repository

**Laxmapuram** isn't just a pin on a map.

It's a village that has watched centuries move тАФ through harvests, festivals, monsoons, and migrations. Nestled in **Ramannapet Mandal**, **Yadadri District**, in the heart of **Telangana**, Laxmapuram carries the quiet pride of rural India: deep roots, hardworking people, and a culture that hasn't asked for the world's attention тАФ but absolutely deserves it.

This repository exists to change that.

We are building a **digital home** for Laxmapuram тАФ a place where residents, emigrants, historians, and curious minds can discover the village's identity, heritage, people, and pulse. For the child who grew up here and moved to Hyderabad. For the researcher studying Telangana's rural geography. For the neighbor who just wants to know more. For anyone who believes that **small places can have big stories**.
