Submit Your Photos

Help us build a living archive of Laxmapuram

📸 Share Your Laxmapuram Memories

Do you have photographs of the village, temples, festivals, or everyday life in Laxmapuram? We'd love to feature them in this gallery! Submit your photos via GitHub and help us create a rich visual record of our village.

📂 Submit on GitHub

How to Contribute Photos

  1. Visit the GitHub repository at github.com/Laxmapuram/Laxmapuram and click "Fork" to create your own copy.
  2. Create a new folder under content/images/ with a descriptive name (e.g., festivals/bathukamma-2024/).
  3. Add your photos (JPG or PNG, max 5MB each). Please ensure faces are not shown without consent.
  4. Add a README.md in the folder with photo descriptions, year, and photographer credit.
  5. Submit a Pull Request with a clear description. Our volunteers will review and merge it.

📋 Photo Guidelines: Please share photos in good resolution (at least 1200px wide). Avoid sharing photos with personally identifiable information without consent. Historical photos and documentary images of festivals, landmarks, and agriculture are especially valued. By submitting, you grant permission for photos to be used on this website under a Creative Commons attribution licence.
