Gram Panchayat

Local self-governance at the village level

🏛️ Laxmapuram Gram Panchayat

  • Panchayat Name Laxmapuram Gram Panchayat
  • Mandal Ramannapet Mandal
  • District Yadadri Bhuvanagiri
  • Head Sarpanch (elected)
  • Panchayat Secretary As per government roster
  • Ward Members Elected ward members

The Gram Panchayat is the primary unit of local self-government in Laxmapuram. It is responsible for village roads, drainage, drinking water, sanitation, streetlights, and welfare schemes. Panchayat elections are held every five years.

Sanitation & Swachh Bharat

A clean village, a healthy community

🚿 Open Defecation Free (ODF)

Laxmapuram has been declared an Open Defecation Free (ODF) village under the Swachh Bharat Mission (Clean India Mission). Every household has access to a toilet, and the village maintains its ODF status through community awareness and monitoring.

♻️ Solid Waste Management

The Gram Panchayat manages solid waste collection and disposal. Households are encouraged to segregate wet and dry waste. The village takes pride in maintaining clean streets, especially during festival seasons like Bathukamma and Ugadi when the village is specially decorated.

🌿 Palle Pragathi

Under the Telangana Government's Palle Pragathi programme, Laxmapuram has undertaken village beautification, plantation drives, and road improvement works. Community participation in these activities has strengthened the sense of ownership and civic pride among residents.
