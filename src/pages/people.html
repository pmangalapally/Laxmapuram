Our Community

The heart of every village is its people

Laxmapuram is home to approximately 2,000–3,000 people spread across 400–600 households. The village community includes farmers, teachers, government servants, small business owners, and daily wage workers — all bound together by shared faith, festivals, and a deep love for their homeland.

This page honours the individuals who have contributed to the village's growth, education, culture, and welfare. We invite all villagers and their descendants to share profiles and stories.

🌟 Village's Most Prominent Figure

A son of Laxmapuram who made the village proud

PKM

Pavan Kumar Reddy Mangalapally

పవన్ కుమార్ రెడ్డి మంగళపల్లి

Technology Professional & Village Ambassador

⭐ Prominent Figure

Pavan Kumar Reddy Mangalapally is one of the most distinguished sons of Laxmapuram. Born and raised in the village, Pavan went on to build an accomplished career in technology, carrying the spirit of Laxmapuram with him every step of the way. He is widely recognised as the driving force behind this very digital initiative — creating an online presence for the village so that Laxmapuram's story reaches every corner of the world.

Pavan's journey from the fields and lanes of Laxmapuram to the world of software and technology is an inspiration to every young person in the village. His deep connection to his roots, his respect for the elders, and his commitment to giving back to the community make him the embodiment of Laxmapuram's values — hard work, humility, and a love for the land.

Through his initiative, Laxmapuram has a digital home on GitHub Pages — a living website that documents the village's history, temples, festivals, civic infrastructure, and people for future generations. Pavan continues to champion the village's development and actively encourages fellow villagers across the globe to contribute their stories and knowledge to this platform.
